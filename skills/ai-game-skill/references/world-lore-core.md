# World Lore Core

Use this file as the clean working summary of the project's world lore.

Reason:
- the source lore document in the repo contains encoding damage in places
- future agents still need a stable version of the rules for implementation decisions

## Core Myth

The world is shaped by two ancient evolutionary philosophies:
- devour: power through seizure and assimilation
- fusion: power through connection and symbiosis

The ancient gods were not fully destroyed. Their complete divinity was shattered into fragments of authority and entered ruins, gyms, guardians, and old rituals.

Important implication:
- devour and fusion do not create new gods from nothing
- they are competing methods of restoring sleeping divine power

## Authority Rules

Authority is not a machine part or ordinary crafting material.
It is a fragment of ancient divinity.

Operational rule:
- gyms and guardians do not hand out authority casually
- authority must be recognized through trial and resonance

## Gym Rule

This is one of the most important project rules:

`A badge proves trainer strength. It does not equal inheritance of divine authority.`

Use this rule whenever writing:
- gym dialogue
- story progression
- trial systems
- legendary or divine evolution hooks

## Three Evolution Paths

The world supports three long-term paths:
- normal evolution
- fusion evolution
- devour evolution

Design rule:
- special evolution must not simply replace normal evolution
- all three paths should remain competitively and narratively meaningful

## Competitive Balance Intent

The target ecosystem is coexistence, not domination.

Desired result:
- normal evolution remains mainstream
- fusion and devour remain powerful but conditional
- strategy matters more than raw inflation

## Fusion Rules

- dual-body fusion is the mainstream stable form
- three-body or higher fusion is exceptional and should not be standard progression
- low-rank individuals fuse more easily
- high-rank individuals are harder to fuse cleanly
- the stronger the individual, the fewer fusion bodies it can stably carry

## Devour Rules

- devour is a power path through seizure and assimilation
- it should feel dangerous and decisive
- it should not become a simple "always stronger" upgrade lane

## Apex and Legendary Separation

Keep this hierarchy clean:
- normal
- rare
- Apex
- Legendary

Implementation rule:
- Apex is powerful regional dominance
- Legendary is a separate narrative and system tier
- never merge the two in reward language or story role

## Sprint-Safe Narrative Guidance

For the first-gym demo:
- badges prove training worth
- true authority still requires trial
- divine-scale payoff is future-facing, not immediate

If a story beat makes the player feel like beating one gym instantly grants god-tier power, it is off-lore.
